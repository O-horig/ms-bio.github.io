ms-bio.info

質量分析インフォマティクス研究会


This website is run by "Mass Spec Informatics SIG," a Special Interest Group of JSBi (Japanese Society for Bioinformatics). The purpose of this website (and this SIG) is the acceleration of interactions between research communities within the fields of mass spectrometry and informatics. The official language in this site is Japanese; all the following pages are also optimized for the Japanese language environment, and thus only pages in Japanese are available at the current time.
